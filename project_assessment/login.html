Welcome to HCL Student Portal

The HCL Student Portal is an online platform created to support students in their learning and career journey. Through this portal, students can stay updated with training programs, skill development courses, certifications, and placement-related information provided by HCL.

Portal Features

  • Access to enrolled courses
  • Training schedules and learning materials
  • Certification and assessment updates
  • Career guidance and placement support

Who Can Login?

  • Registered students
  • HCL training program participants
  • Internship candidates
